How Full Body Laser Hair Removal Works

Curious about how Full body laser hair removal El Paso works? Let’s break it down.

The procedure involves the use of concentrated light energy, also known as laser energy, to target the hair follicle. The laser emits light, which is absorbed by the pigment in the hair. The energy from the light heats up the hair follicle and inhibits future hair growth. Over time, the treated hair will shed, leaving you with smooth skin.

The laser technology works best on hair in the active growth phase, which is why multiple treatments are required to ensure all hair is effectively targeted. Typically, clients need between 6-8 sessions to achieve optimal results. After completing the full course of treatments, you’ll notice a significant reduction in hair growth, and the hair that does grow back will be finer and lighter.
